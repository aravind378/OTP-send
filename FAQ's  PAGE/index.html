What Can We Help You Find?

DAY CARE

At NSKC Children’s Day Care, we focus on creating a safe, nurturing, and stimulating environment that supports each child’s individual growth through play-based learning, experienced caregivers, and strong family partnerships.

We provide care for children aged 6 months to 6 years.

We maintain a low staff-to-child ratio to ensure personalized attention: typically 1 staff member per 5 toddlers and 1 per 8 preschoolers.

Our curriculum includes age-appropriate educational activities, creative arts, outdoor play, and early literacy and numeracy skills.

Yes, nutritious meals and snacks are provided daily. We accommodate special dietary needs upon prior notice.

Our facility is secured with controlled access, CCTV monitoring, and all staff are trained in first aid and emergency procedures.

For the safety of all children, we ask that sick children remain at home until they are symptom-free for at least 24 hours.

Yes, we welcome parents to visit. For safety and comfort reasons, extended stays require prior approval.

Yes, children have scheduled nap times with comfortable rest areas suited to their age.

We provide daily reports, regular updates, and are always available for meetings or calls to discuss your child’s progress.

Enrollment requires a registration form, immunization records, and a deposit. Our fee structure varies based on the child’s age and schedule; please contact us for details.

Our trained staff encourage positive interactions, teach conflict resolution skills, and foster a nurturing environment that supports each child’s emotional growth.

TUTION

We offer one-to-one tuitions, private group tuitions, and subject-wise specialized tuition tailored to each student’s needs.

We provide tuition for a wide range of subjects including Math, Science, English, Social Studies, and more, from primary to higher secondary levels.

Our tutors are highly qualified professionals with extensive teaching experience and expertise in their respective subjects.

Yes, we allow students and parents to select tutors based on subject expertise, teaching style, and availability.

We offer both online and in-person tuition options to suit your convenience and learning preferences.

Sessions are customized based on the student’s learning pace, syllabus requirements, and specific goals, typically lasting 1-2 hours.

Yes, we conduct an initial assessment to identify the student’s strengths and areas for improvement to tailor the lessons effectively.

Fees vary depending on the type of tuition (one-to-one, group, subject-wise), session length, and frequency. Please contact us for detailed pricing.

Yes, sessions can be rescheduled with advance notice. Cancellation policies depend on the specific arrangement with your tutor.

Tutors provide regular feedback and progress reports to students and parents to ensure continuous improvement.

We provide tuition for students from elementary school through to higher secondary and pre-university levels.

Enrollment is simple—contact us via phone, email, or visit our center to discuss your needs and schedule your first session.

SKILL DEVELOPMENT COURSE

We offer courses in Yoga, Spoken English, Spoken Hindi, Hindi, Abacus, Reading, Handwriting, Vedic Maths, Bharatanatyam, Karate, Chess, Computer, Drawing, Carnatic Music, and Slogan Practice

Our courses are open to children and adults of all ages, with specific classes designed for different age groups and skill levels.

We provide both in-person and online classes to accommodate different learning preferences and schedules.

Course durations vary; typically, sessions last between 45 minutes to 1.5 hours, with flexible schedules available.

Yes, courses are structured to cater to beginners, intermediate, and advanced learners depending on the subject.

Our instructors are trained professionals and experts in their respective fields with experience in teaching all age groups.

Yes, students can enroll in multiple courses simultaneously based on availability and schedule.

Fees vary by course and duration. Please contact us directly for detailed pricing and any available discounts.

Some courses include assessments, and we provide certificates of completion to acknowledge your achievements.

Required materials vary by course; some may require special equipment or supplies, which will be communicated during enrollment.

Enrollment can be done by visiting our center, calling us, or through our website with a simple registration process.

Yes, we offer trial or demo classes for many of our courses so you can experience the teaching style before committing.

ONLINE CRASH COURSE

We offer intensive crash courses in popular programming languages (like Python, Java, C++) and design tools (such as Photoshop, Illustrator, and UI/UX design).

Our crash courses typically last between 2 to 6 weeks, designed to cover essential concepts quickly and effectively.

The courses are primarily live sessions with interactive classes, but some may include self-paced modules for flexible learning.

You will need a computer or laptop with a stable internet connection, and for design courses, software access may be required (which we will guide you to install).

Yes, participants who successfully complete the course receive a certificate recognized by NSKC Academy.

Enrollment is easy—simply contact us via phone, email, or through our website to choose your course and schedule.
